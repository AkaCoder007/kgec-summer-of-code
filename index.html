KGEC Summer of Code

A program focused on bringing more students in the field of development and open source, KGEC Summer of Code, organized by Google DSC KGEC, provides a platform for students to connect, learn and build projects with other student developers in a 2-month long learning extensive period.

    • About the program

    GDSC KGEC is organizing a program similar to, but not quite the same as Google Summer of Code, providing students the opportunity to participate and build projects in teams, for a period of two months.
